About Us

At Make I.T. Basic, we believe technology should empower, not overwhelm. Based in Tyler, Texas, we are a local IT company dedicated to reshaping the industry with a fresh approach to Managed IT services, computer repair, and all our technology services.

With over 20 years of diverse experience across the IT industry, our team brings deep expertise and a passion for helping businesses thrive. We know that IT doesn’t need to be overly complicated or expensive. That’s why we focus on making technology accessible, affordable, and, most importantly, effective for our clients.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple

Make IT work for you. We partner with our clients as a trusted extension of their team, offering proactive solutions that prioritize security, productivity, and growth. Unlike others who wait for issues to arise, we actively seek opportunities to optimize your operations and advance your goals.

What Sets Us Apart

Security-Focused Managed Services

In today’s digital landscape, security is non-negotiable. We prioritize protecting your business while ensuring your IT infrastructure supports seamless productivity.

Affordable IT Solutions

We’re here to break the mold of overcharging. Our goal is to deliver high-quality services that fit within your budget, ensuring that cutting-edge IT is within reach for all businesses.

Client Empowerment Through Education

We don’t just fix problems; we empower our clients with the knowledge and tools to succeed. By demystifying technology, we help you feel confident and in control.

Why Choose Us?

When you choose Make I.T. Basic, you’re not just getting a service provider—you’re gaining a proactive partner who’s invested in your success. Whether it’s streamlining your daily operations or preparing you for future challenges, we’re here to ensure that technology becomes your greatest asset.

Let’s make IT simple, secure, and impactful—together.
